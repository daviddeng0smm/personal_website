My Journey

As a first-generation immigrant and a dedicated student at the University of California, Irvine, where I am currently pursuing a degree in software engineering, my journey has been marked by determination, innovation, and a relentless pursuit of excellence.

Before embarking on my academic journey at UC Irvine, I was an entrepreneur, founding and managing several successful brands on Amazon. One of these ventures, "Home Penguin," specializes in high-quality construction tools such as contour gauges and hex wrenches, designed to meet the needs of both professionals and DIY enthusiasts. Another brand, "T-Nature," focuses on outdoor waterproof blankets, catering to nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts.

My entrepreneurial spirit also led me to manage an Airbnb property, where I took on the roles of both a designer and a coordinator. I personally oversaw the renovation, ensuring every detail was meticulously planned and executed, and organized professional photo shoots to highlight the unique features of the property.

Since deciding to pursue a degree in technology, I have had the honor of being named an Apple Pathway Scholar, which has provided me with invaluable resources and mentorship. Additionally, I landed an internship at MetLife, gaining practical experience in the industry. Currently, I am a Software Engineer Fellow at HeadStarter, where I continue to hone my skills and contribute to innovative projects.

Now, as I immerse myself in the world of software engineering, I am eager to gain more hands-on experience in the tech industry. My goal is to combine my entrepreneurial background with my technical skills to eventually launch my own startup, where I can bring innovative solutions to life and make a lasting impact in the tech world.

This blend of academic rigor, entrepreneurial experience, and a forward-looking vision defines my professional journey, and I am excited to continue this path, embracing new challenges and opportunities along the way.
